About Us

A family-owned and operated business, we are proud to serve the communities of Gwinnett County and to provide clients with high-quality and efficient electrical solutions. We are dedicated to a job well done, a streamlined process, and clients' peace of mind. From troubleshooting to complete rewires, you can count on us every step of the way for your project.

Services

We provide the full range of residential electrical services. Starting with the installation of outlets, new lights, fans, or multi-location switches, we take on larger projects like remodeling a bathroom, a kitchen, a basement, or the entire house. We specialize in LED lighting, electrical panel upgrades, electric vehicle chargers, generator and transfer switch installation. And we do everything with the knowledge we gained over the years, tested in dozens of successful projects for happy customers.

Lighting

We install any kind of light, to make your home more pleasant and your life brighter. Chandeliers, recessed lighting, sconces, fixtures, flood-lights, track lights… anything.

Electrical Panel Upgrades

We can upgrade your electrical meter and panel and bring more electrical power into your home. Our complete electrical service upgrade includes a new panel, new meter, new grounding system, new breakers, and new service entry wire.

Electric Vehicle Chargers

The EV charger installation service includes everything from determining the best location for the charger to installing it and testing it to make sure it works correctly.
